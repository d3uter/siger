Welcome to Signature Fixer...

Signature Fixer will look for a PREFIX and SUFFIX string, above and below your signature. It will automatically remove the code found inbetween these strings. Just add the strings you have entered here into the top and bottom of your signature and it will enable the automatic removal of your signature when you change the FROM field.

NOTE: On sending, Signature Fixer will automatically remove the PREFIX and SUFFIX strings from your email message.

HOW TO USE:
1. INSTALL the 'Signature Fixer' addon and open the OPTIONS dialog.
2. CHECK/UPDATE the 'Signature Prefix' and 'Signature Suffix' options.
3. EDIT your signature (HTML or plain text, it doesnt matter).
4. ADD the 'Signature Prefix' to the very top of your signature.
5. ADD the 'Signature Suffix' to the very bottom of your signature.
